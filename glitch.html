 About this work: 
    
The pictures above are from the military parades of East Germany and North Korea, as well as the Soviet Union. 
    
The first one uses the Text Editor, the second uses Audacity, and the third uses a combination of the two. 
    
The reason why I chose these images is that I personally like this communist art culture, 
    
And the use of glitch art can add to this "mysterious" atmosphere even more.
