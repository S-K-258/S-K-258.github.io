About this work:

My image is an attempt to draw a "Spider Solitaire" victory picture similar to the one that comes with the Windows 7 system.

The thing I made was inspired by Spider Solitaire.  

Yes, it's a fake card simulator. 

You can use playing cards to do anything, such as drawing Mobius strips and filling your screen with chips. 

Even draw an anime character using playing cards! ! ! (Actually, it’s very limited, because I didn’t make all the playing cards, only a few)
