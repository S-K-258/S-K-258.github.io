"CS2 training simulator (catch the ball update)"





    Instructions for use:
  
1. When custom mode is enabled, the button will turn green, indicating that you are using custom mode, if not, 
  
it is not in use.
  
2. When starting the custom mode, clicking the reset ball once will only reset the position of the ball, 
  
and clicking it a second time will reset all parameters.
  
3. And enjoy it !!!
