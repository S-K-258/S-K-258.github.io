About this work:

I decided to make a trophy about my favorite car. 

Mitsubishi cars are like my playmates from childhood to adulthood. 

My first exposure to Mitsubishi Motors was a long time ago through Jackie Chan movies and the initials D.

Later, when I was in middle school,my math teacher had a Lancer Evolution 9 and later I watched the WRC competition. 

I made this trophy not only out of pure love but also to commemorate its last immortal legend. 

I hope there will be new Lancer Evolution in the future.
