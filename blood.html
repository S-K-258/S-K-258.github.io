About this work:

Blood is a short film that blends Cartoon drawing/animation with historical and contemporary military footage to

explore the destructive power of anger and violence between nations.

Set against the backdrop of events such as the Gulf War,

the war in Afghanistan, the war in Ukraine and the collapse of the Soviet Union,

the film highlights the cyclical nature of conflict and its cost in human lives. Victor Tsoi's score,

"Blood Type," emphasizes the shared pain and humanity behind these geopolitical struggles,

making Blood a heartfelt and thought-provoking reflection on Wrath and its consequences.
